Instructions

The Game starts ONLY with a HIT.

You can press HIT until you get BUST.

STAND can be pressed only after a HIT, similarly DEAL can be pressed only after a STAND.

Press RESET to reset the game.

How to play!

Start pressing HIT until score is closer to less than or equal to 21.

Player gets BUSTED if score crosses 21.

Now press STAND so that DEALER can play.

BUSTED

If YOU and DEALER both get BUSTED then it's a draw.

If YOU get BUSTED and DEALER don't then it's a loss.
NOT BUSTED

If YOUR score is more than DEALER score then it's a win.

If YOUR score is less than DEALER score then it's a loss.

If YOUR score is equal to DEALER score then it's a draw.
